In Islamic history, the last Islamic khilafat is the ottoman empire. The ottoman empire was founded by ‘Osman Gazi.’ The term ‘ottoman’ derived from Osman’s name. It’s raised in the late 13th century and collapsed in the early 20th century. Osman was the bey of the ‘Kaye’ tribe. This tribe settled in Anatolia after scaping from Central Asia as the consequence of the Mongol offensive.
